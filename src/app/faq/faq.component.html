Account FAQ:


How do I register for a new account?

Step 1: Navigate to the Sign In page.

Step 2: Click the Create New Account button.



Step 3: Fill out all form fields and pick three security questions to answer (these will be used in case you need to reset your password in the future) then click the submit button. You will automatically be redirected back to the Sign In page.



Step 4: Sign in using the email address and password you entered from Step 3.
How do I reset my password?

Step 1: Navigate to the Sign In page.

Step 2: Click the Forgot Password button.



Step 4: Enter the email address you used when you registered as a user (and that you use to sign in with) into the input bar and click the Verify Account button.



Step 4: Enter the answers to your security questions and click the Verify Security Questions button. All three answers must be correct in order to reset your password.



Step 5: Enter your new password and click the Change Password button. You will be automatically redirected to the Sign In page where you can now login with your new password.

















Customer Service FAQ:


How do I make an invoice?

Step 1: Navigate to the Add Invoice page.

Step 2: Fill out the Customer Information (Customer Email, Customer Full Name).



Step 3: Fill out the Invoice Date (must be in the yyyy-mm-dd format).



Step 4: Select all fixed cost services that the customer has requested by checking the applicable check boxes.



Step 5: If the customer has any custom work that they want performed, enter the total number of hours (fractional hours i.e. "1.5" are okay) the work will take and the total cost for the parts (if applicable) for this custom work.



Step 6: Click the "Create Invoice" button at the bottom of the form. The button will be greyed out (disabled) if there are any errors or missing items on the form. Once you hit the button, you will automatically be redirected to the Invoice List page where you will see your newly created invoice.
How do I print an invoice?

Step 1: Navigate to the Invoice List page.

Step 2: Search the entries for the invoice you wish to print. If you know the customer email or custom name, hit ctrl-f to search to find it easier.

Step 3: Click the yellow Invoice Number button for the invoice you wish to print. This will redirect you automatically to the invoice view page for that individual invoice.



Step 4: Click the print icon on the invoice. This will initiate the computer's print function. All background colors and unnecessary formatting will automatically be hidden in order to print a clean invoice for the customer.



Step 5: Click print on your computer for the printer that you wish to use.

















Administrator FAQ:


How do I add a user?

Step 1: Navigate to the User List page - the link is located in the top right of the page under the profile icon when you are signed in as an Administrator.



Step 2: On the User List page, click the "Add A New User" button. This will bring you to the User Creationpage.



Step 3: On the User Creation page, fill out the form with the New User's Information. Note: Ensure all fields are filled out and in the proper format or you will receive an error. The submit button will not be active until all errors are resolved.

Step 4: Hit the Submit button at the bottom of the form. You will then be redirected back to the User List page where you should see your newly created User!


How do I edit a user's information?

Step 1: Navigate to the User List page - the link for this is located in the top right of the page under the profile icon when you are signed in as an Administrator.



Step 2: Click the pencil icon next to the user you wish to disable.



Step 3: After clicking the pencil icon you will be directed to the Update View page. This page will be populated with the current user information. Change any information here that you want to update and then click the Submit button. You will then be redirected back to the User List page where your updated record will be displayed.


How do I disable a user?

Step 1: Navigate to the User List page - the link is located in the top right of the page under the profile icon when you are signed in as an Administrator.



Step 2: Click the trash can icon next to the user you wish to disable.



Step 3: After clicking the trash can icon, a dialog box will ask you to confirm that you want to disable the user. Click OK.



Step 4: After clicking OK, you should see a User Disabled Successfully message and then the page will refresh after a few seconds and the user that was disabled should no longer be visible.
